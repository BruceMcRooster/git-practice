The main course of the meal will consist of:
A taco bar, including beef, turkey, and Beyond meat options,
shredded lettuce, diced tomato, black olives, and red peppers,
along with guacamole, salsa, and mango salsa.
Cheeses, both dairy and non-dairy options, are also available.

For those who need a lot to eat, steamed brown and jasmine rice should be provided to make taco salads.
